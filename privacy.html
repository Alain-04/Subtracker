Privacy Policy

At SubTrack, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data while providing you with the best subscription tracking experience possible.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information (like your name and email) and subscription-related data to help you manage your accounts efficiently. We also collect anonymous usage data for analytics purposes.

2. Use of Data

Your information is used to personalize your experience, provide customer support, and improve SubTrack’s performance. We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

3. Security

All sensitive information is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use modern cloud security standards to protect against unauthorized access.

4. Your Rights

You can access, modify, or delete your data by contacting us at support@subtrack.com.

5. Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
